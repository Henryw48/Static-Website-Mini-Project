Hobbies/Interests

Sports

One of my favorite sports is basketball. I used to play a lot during middle school with friends even though I wasn't that good. I could probably work on my shooting form and handles, but I haven't played much cause of work overload. I also like to play volleyball. The main reason I got into volleyball was because I watched an anime called "Haikyu!!". I really like playing up front defending and spiking.

Gaming

I love playing games during my free time. One thing I like about gaming is when my teammates are screaming at each other to do better. Some games I play are counterstrike, valorant, roblox, osu, and occasionally fortnite. Gaming is just not about winning. It's the moments of exploring and collaborating which makes the game fun.
