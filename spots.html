Spots

Central Park

Central park is one of the biggest parks that I have ever been to. There is just so much to explore, however you can easily get lost. I recommend you have either your friends or family if you decide to visit.

High Line

The high line is such a cool experience. There are such amazing views to see and if you are hungry or want to shop, then there is a mall nearby to satisfy all your needs and wants. I have went to the highline twice and both times were very fun.

Little Island

Little Island is near the highline and it is basically a vessel. It is a man made island that has great scenery, perfect for taking high quality photos. I went with my sister and we took alot of photos together.
